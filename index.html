Corgi Runner

Story

You are Dio Jr, son of the legendary heavy metal vocalist. Your beloved corgi Ozzy the Second has run away because you didn't play his favorite song Holy Diver. Catch your corgi so you can perform the greatest song of all time to your best friend.

How to Play

Choose a difficulty Easy, Medium, Hard. Harder difficulty gets you more points!

Press Space to jump and avoid obstacles.

Jump over trees.

Don't jump into birds! (Only spawns on Hard)
